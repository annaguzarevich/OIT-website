Bioinformatics

Where Biology becomes a Big Data problem.

Biology is no longer just about microscopes and petri dishes. Since the sequencing of the Human Genome, biology has transformed into an information science.

Bioinformatics is the field where Computer Science, Statistics, and Biology converge to analyze the massive datasets generated by modern science.
